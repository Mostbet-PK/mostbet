Why does the name on registration need to match the ID exactly?

Most platforms compare the profile data against the verification document at the first cashout. Even small spelling differences can put a payout on hold until the gap is resolved through the help desk.

Can a registration be edited later if some data is wrong?

Basic edits are usually possible inside the account settings, while changes to the legal name or date of birth typically go through customer support with a copy of the ID attached to the request.

Is opting into the welcome bonus required at sign-up?

No. The welcome offer is a separate opt-in, and skipping it during registration is a valid choice for players who prefer to deposit without a wagering window attached to the balance.

How long does the first verification usually take after registration?

Timing varies by platform and by the document submitted, yet a clean ID image and a matching profile usually move the review along faster than blurry scans or mismatched data.
