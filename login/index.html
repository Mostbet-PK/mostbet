Short sign-in questions from PK readers

What happens when the password is forgotten?

The account area lists an official reset route. It is the only path that should be used, since random reset links arriving from outside emails or messengers may not be tied to the platform at all.

Why does the session sometimes expire mid-game?

Most platforms apply a session timeout after a period of inactivity or after a fixed total length. A fresh sign-in restores the same account state, with any unfinished game returned to the lobby.

Can the same account be used in the app and the browser at the same time?

In most cases the latest session takes priority and the older one is asked to sign in again. This behaviour is part of the standard credential hygiene applied across the platform.

Is two-step verification worth turning on?

Yes, whenever the option is available. The extra step adds a barrier on top of the password itself, which lowers the chance of an outside login attempt succeeding.
